Created on Wed May 1 19:05:53 2019

@author: yoelr

biosteam.units.decorators.cost(basis, name=None, *, cost, exp, CE, S=1, kW=0, N=1)[source]¶

Add item purchase cost based on exponential scale up:

\(C_{f.o.b.} = (N)(CE)(cost)(\frac{basis}{S})^{exp}\)

\(Electricity\ rate = (N)(kW)(\frac{basis}{S})\)

Parameters

basis: Name of Design item used for scaling.

name: Name of item.

cost: Cost of item.

exp: Exponential factor.

CE: Chemical engineering plant cost index.

S: Size.

kW: Electricity rate.

N: Number of items or name of Design item that represents N.
