MixTank¶

class biosteam.MixTank(ID='', outs=(), ins=None, **kwargs)[source]¶

Create a mixing tank with volume based on residence time.

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}V &= \frac{\tau* Q}{0.8}\\C_{fob}^{2007} &= 12080 * V^{0.525} (0.1 < V < 30 m^3)\end{aligned}\end{align} \]
